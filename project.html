Pollution

Pollution is the introduction of harmful external impurities into the environment. All forms of pollution destroy the environment and negatively affect fauna and flora. There are three main types of pollution; land, air,and water pollution.

Land Pollution

Also known as land degradation, this type of pollution destroys the soil and vegetative cover of the land. Examples of activities that degrade the land include:

  • Sand winning

  • Deforestation

  • Overgrazing

  • Mining

Water Pollution

Water pollution is the addition of impurities into waterbodies, making them unfit for use. Examples of activities that destroy waterbodies are:

  • Dumping solid and liquid waste into waterbodies

  • Fishing with chemicals

  • Oil spiils from leaking ships
    Mining

Air Pollution

Air pollutionis making the air unsafe to inhale for living organisms. Examples of activties that contribute to air pollution are as follows:

  • Smoke from wildfires

  • Smoke from vehicle exhaust pipes

  • Smoke from industries
